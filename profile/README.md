# Aachen Data Science Club e.V. 

Student initiative at RWTH Aachen University focused on Data Science, Machine Learning, and Artificial Intelligence. 

## About 
ADSC connects students across disciplines to develop practical skills in data science through workshops, collaborative projects, and industry partnerships.
